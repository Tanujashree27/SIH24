Cold storage plays a vital role in preserving the quality of fruits and vegetables. It ensures that products stay fresh for a longer period of time by maintaining an optimal temperature and humidity level. The facilities are equipped with modern technology that helps control the environment inside the storage chambers.

These storage systems help in reducing spoilage and wastage of perishable goods, thus increasing the overall shelf life of fruits. High-quality cold storage facilities ensure that fruits retain their taste, texture, and nutritional value for weeks or even months after being harvested.

With cold storage, farmers and sellers can offer a continuous supply of fruits, ensuring their customers receive only the freshest products, no matter the season. The facilities often include temperature control, humidity management, and monitoring systems to ensure optimal storage conditions.
