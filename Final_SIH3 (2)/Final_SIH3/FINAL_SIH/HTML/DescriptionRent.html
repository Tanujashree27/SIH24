Cold Storage Description

This cold storage facility provides state-of-the-art temperature control, ensuring that your crops and goods are preserved under optimal conditions. With large capacity and advanced refrigeration systems, this cold storage is perfect for farmers and businesses looking to store fresh produce for extended periods.

Features & Functionalities

  • Temperature control: -5°C to 10°C
  • 24/7 monitoring and maintenance
  • High capacity: Can store up to 100 tons of produce
  • Advanced humidity control for sensitive crops
  • Eco-friendly and energy-efficient systems
