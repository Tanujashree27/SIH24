Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I register on the platform?

To register, click on the "Sign In/Register" button at the top right of the page and follow the instructions to create an account.

How can I list my products for sale?

After logging in, go to the "Sell" section and follow the prompts to list your products with detailed descriptions and pricing.

How do I rent cold storage space?

Visit the "Rent" section, enter your location, and explore available cold storage spaces. Once you find one, follow the instructions to reserve it.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and mobile wallets.

How do I track the freshness and expiry of my products?

Our AI-powered tool helps you track the freshness and expiry of your products automatically once you list them on the platform.
